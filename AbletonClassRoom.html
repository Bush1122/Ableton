Ableton for the Classroom

Spark a Love for Making Music

Inspire young people to learn music through the excitement of making their own.

Get instruments, support and ready-to-use resources for your education organization – and put the thrill of making music at the center of your class.
Why teach with Live and Push?

Connect learning with the music that matters to young people. Explore key concepts with a captivated classroom. And inspire learners with the belief they can create their own music.

Offers for schools

Get free Ableton Live Intro licenses for classes with young learners, and get significant discounts on Push 2 and Live Suite. And apply for free Push 2 units if your organization has a low budget for instruments..
