Training at Ableton

Ableton offers training in the area of ​​office management (office management clerk). On this page you will find all information about our training program.

Dual training system

Training programs have a long tradition in Germany and, like studying, are a popular educational path. Dual training allows you to practically apply the theoretical knowledge you learned at vocational school in a company and to develop your personal and professional skills. You attend vocational school twice a week and are at the company on the other working days.

This is what training at Ableton looks like

We integrate you into our processes as a full team member right from the start and assign you responsible tasks. During the two and a half years of your training, you will get to know a total of up to 5 stations in which you will work independently on projects in addition to recurring tasks. Ableton values ​​its feedback culture, which is why you receive regular feedback during your training and have the opportunity to express your wishes and thoughts. Together with your trainer and your mentors, you will regularly analyze your strengths and development goals in order to advance your personal and professional development. To make it easier for you to get started, you will be accompanied and supported by a trainee in all your questions through our buddy program. We also guarantee you a fair training salary, a BVG ticket, internal training opportunities and much more.

Your profile:

In addition to your great interest in office administration and a completed (technical) university entrance qualification or secondary school leaving certificate, you ideally have the following qualities:

  • Motivation, commitment and ability to learn
  • Organizational skills and independence
  • Teamwork skills
  • Communication skills and open-mindedness
  • Confident use of Microsoft 365 (especially Word, Excel)
  • very good knowledge of German and English, both spoken and written

Submit application:

You can apply to start your training on September 1st (application period March to May) or February 1st (application period August to October).

Your application:

Here you will find all the important information for your application documents:

  • Motivation letter
  • CV (without photo)
  • last school certificate (if applicable, high school diploma)
  • Further references (e.g. internship certificates or, if necessary, language certificates)
